About MDP MUN

Welcome to the Global Edge School's MDP Model United Nations. Our MUN conference aims to provide students with a platform to engage in diplomatic discussions, develop their public speaking skills, and gain a deeper understanding of global issues.

Schedule

Our MUN conference will be held over three days, with various sessions and activities planned for each day. Stay tuned for the detailed schedule.
